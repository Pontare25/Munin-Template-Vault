%% #AI-Assisted %%

Ten minutes of review spread over five days builds more durable memory than fifty minutes in one sitting. The forgetting curve resets stronger each time material is recalled after a gap; without the gap, repetition mostly re-reads what is still in short-term memory.

Practical consequence: schedule reviews, do not extend sessions.
