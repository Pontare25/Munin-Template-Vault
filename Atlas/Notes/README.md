# Atlas/Notes

Atomic notes: one idea per note, stated in your own words (or the AI's), linked to where it came from and what it connects to. This is the Zettelkasten core of the vault. Small notes with many links beat long documents with none, because links are what the AI and the graph can follow.

## What belongs here

- One idea, claim, argument, or observation per note.
- Frontmatter: `type: note`, `up:` (at least one link to a Topic or parent idea), `created-date:`. `topics:`, `related:`, `down:`, `challenges:` encouraged.
- A `%% #AI-Assisted %%` marker at the top when the AI wrote it.

## What does not belong here

- Topic overviews (make a Topic in `Atlas/Topics/`).
- Raw highlights or unprocessed source material (keep in `Raw/` until ingested).
- Long multi-idea documents: split them.
